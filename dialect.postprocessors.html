twitch
module dialect.postprocessors.twitch

The Twitch postprocessor processes dialect.defs.IRCEvents after they are parsed, and deals with Twitch-specifics. Those include extracting the colour someone's name should be printed in, their alias/"display name" (generally their nickname cased), converting the event to some event types unique to Twitch, etc.
